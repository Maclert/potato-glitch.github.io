Strongest High School Primate

Unlock at Lv10

A young man known as the "Strongest High School Primate" in another world.
Spending nearly 4000 years in a petrified state, he took down a lion with his bare hands moments after being revived. Tsukasa originally cooperated with Senku and his friends, but they went their separate paths.
The scientist was determined to bring back all of humanity, whereas the fighter wanted to go with the soft reset and make a new world without modern technology for the pure-hearted.
He obtained the recipe for the revival fluid and started a kingdom of ones he selected to be depetrified.
