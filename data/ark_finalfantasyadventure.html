At the beginning of everything...

Unlock at Lv2

The peak of Mount Irushia soars above the clouds...
Upon its top lies enshrined the Tree of Mana.
The Tree perceives all power within the universe, converting it to energy which it uses to nourish itself. According to legend, those who touch the tree may gain eternal power...

The Shadow Knight, ruler of the Glance Dukedom, sought the power of the Tree of Mana in his quest to subjugate the world in terror and endless slaughter.
