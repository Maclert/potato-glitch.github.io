Young Scientist by Nature

Unlock at Lv10

A young scientist from another world. Anything mysterious sparked his strong curiosity at a young age; he was constantly interested in making the illogical, logical. Senku grew up tinkering with experiments, technology, and scientific research on a daily basis.

However, one day all of humanity was mysteriously turned to stone. 3719 years later, Senku miraculously awakens to find a “Stone World” where civilization has been destroyed…

Determined to return the world back to what it once was, he uses his encyclopedic knowledge of science to rebuild civilization from scratch with his friends and allies.
