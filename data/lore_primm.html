The Lady from the Kingdom of Pandora

Unlock at Lv10

A lone hero destined for greatness in another world.
The daughter of a minister belonging to the Pandora Kingdom. She lost her mother at a young age and lived alone with her father. Due to her spoiled upbringing in which she wanted for nothing, she became selfish and developed a wilful personality.

She became enamored of an officer in the royal army named Dyluck, but thanks to the machinations of her father, who preferred to see her married off to a noble family of standing, Officer Dyluck was sent away to subjugate a witch living in the Haunted Forest.

The girl was incensed and fled her home to set forth on a journey to seek out her beloved.
