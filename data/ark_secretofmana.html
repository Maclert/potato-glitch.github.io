Long ago, a civilization which used the power of Mana flourished.
A fierce battle between a massive craft created using the power of Mana, known as the Mana Fortress, and Mana Beasts sent by the gods, engulfed the world in flames, spreading poison throughout the land.

As the power of Mana started to fade, the fortress was razed by a brave warrior carrying the Mana Sword, after which the Mana Beasts disappeared and peace was restored to the world.

History repeats as time flows onward...
