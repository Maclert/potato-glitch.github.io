Civilization from Scratch

Unlock at Lv2

In a world different from Granzelia, a mysterious phenomenon occurred and civilization had been destroyed in less than a day. With a flash of light and no means of resistance, all of humanity was turned to stone.

After the disappearance of humans, nature began to return to earth and grind down the buildings humanity had made. The world gradually became a paradise for vegetation and animals, as the shadow of a once prosperous civilization slowly faded away over time…

After nearly 4000 years after humanity was gone from earth…
A young genius scientist was the first to awaken and break free of his stone prison, into the Stone World. He soon decides to rebuild civilization from scratch with the power of science.
Around the same period, his old friend, known for his unlimited stamina and power, also starts to awaken. With his friends and new comrades, together they prepare to recreate civilization from scratch…
