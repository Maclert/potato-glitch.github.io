Blonde Hair and Blue Eyes

Unlock at Lv10

A resident and one of the best fighters of Ishigami Village. She is agile and experienced in combat because of her skills in hunting as well as sparring with the other members of the village.

One day, as she was fetching water from the hot springs, she bore witness to a “gentleman sorcerer” being mercilessly killed by a powerful man. Later she confronts and challenges the man, resulting in being stuck under a fallen tree. As she was immobile, the “gentleman sorcerer” she had witnessed earlier saves her.

Over time, she helps out Senku and the other villagers to start the foundation of the Kingdom of Science.
