A door to a sacred land is opened...

Unlock at Lv2

In a time when the world remained enshrouded in darkness, the Mana Goddess, wielding the Mana Sword, brought to heel the eight Mana Beasts (each an incarnation of a calamity which would bring the world to ruin) and sealed the corresponding eight keystones away.
In doing so, she banished the darkness and created a new world.

The Mana Goddess then transformed herself into the form of a tree and fell into a deep slumber lasting many years...
However, those who lusted after power exceeding godhood itself, released the Mana Beasts from the eight keystones and, in an attempt to gain dominion over all the world, brought a great war to the land, ending the enduring peace...
Mana was rapidly depleted from the earth, and the Tree of Mana began to wither...
