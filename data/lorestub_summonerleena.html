Zellpress has retracted its latest release “Leena in Odal” after numerous editorial mistakes were discovered by librarians and readers.
Zellpress has issued a press release with an apology, but refunds are not being accepted. There is no date on when the revised edition will be released.

Activist readers are calling for libraries to boycott Zellpress, citing a long chain of retracted works and unacceptable handling of important historical figures.

Zellpress is a multinational publisher of academic and fantasy literature. Their releases are largely focused on lesser known heroes during the era of the God of Ruin. Zellpress is situated in the Imperial City of Aldana, with branches across Granzelia.
