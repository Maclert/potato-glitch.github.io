A sprite child lost and far from home

Unlock at Lv10

A lone hero destined for greatness in another world.
Originally a child of the sprites, Popoi became lost after being carried away in a great flood that suddenly befell the Sprite Village due to an unprecedented depletion of 'Mana'.

Having barely escaped with their life, the youth, who went by the name of Popoi, reached Gaia's Navel, where they was given aid by a dwarf elder, having lost all memory of who they was and where their home lay.

The naughty and mischievous youth, still a young sprite, earned their keep as an 'exhibit' for passersby and travelers. A stranger in strange land, they dreamed of a day when they might return home...
