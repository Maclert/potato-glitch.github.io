The Fairy of the Spring

Unlock at Lv2

An emerald green spring in a world different from Granzelia.
The spring glows in divine light, and the beauty appears as a mirage of a fairy to some.

But it is as deadly as it is beautiful. It is a force to be reckoned with, a death threat that shouldn't be taken lightly.
The liquid present isn't clear water, but is comprised of sulfuric acid. The splendid spring is a source of poisonous gas that can kill within seconds when inhaled, along with causing severe chemical burns upon contact.

Despite the danger, there were ones that challenged this spring. The genius scientist who awakened from nearly 4000 years of petrification, and his friends. Knowing the dangers, they sought to gather materials from the spring in order to make a medicine.
