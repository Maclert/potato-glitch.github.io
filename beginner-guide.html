Beginner guide

This page consolidates most of the launch advice, Greensburg's beginner's guide, and my experience as a regular in #gl-help on Discord.

If you are a new player, this page was written for you.

This page contains the following:

  • The walkthrough is for all new players up to their first level 80 units.
  • The crystal spending guide has tips on how to maximise crystal efficiency for players who don't spend much, or at all.
  • The reroll guide is for players who want to understand why certain arks are preferred over others for a "starting account". This section is for regular gacha players.

Have questions? Ask in #gl-help on Discord or in the Questions megathread on Reddit.
