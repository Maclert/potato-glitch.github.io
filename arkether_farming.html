Ark ether farming – the abbreviated version

"Ether farming" is when a player repeatedly runs a stage in the shortest time possible to maximise the number of runs done.

The goal is to clear the stage and "lap" around the menu to "Battle Start" in <1 minute. Your time depends on the performance of your device/emulator as well as party optimisation.

Important! Always run with 3 arks equipped! Not equipping arks means wasted runs!

How many runs do I need?

Each stage clear has a 10% chance of increasing ark ether on an Ark by 0.1%. The statistical average is 10,000 runs per 100% ether.

If you no-life or auto-repeat (auto-run), you can max 3 arks in 10-14 days with 8-12h playtime per day.

Basic strategies

Build a mage
(for players without Randi)

Having a built mage (with instant cast accessories for T3 spells) will solve a lot of problems. Like not owning Randi.

If you don't know what kind of mage you want, build a Light mage first because Shining Zok has the highest damage multiplier, there's a ton of Light-weak mobs, and you can farm Imperial Guard Commander 3.

Recommended light mages:

  • Lily (Semi-limited unit)
  • Saintly Theria (Seasonal/Limited unit)
  • Sea Princess Lilah (needs Light Critical + Shining Zok + Light High Boost, Seasonal/Limited unit)
  • Princess Lilah (needs Light High Boost and as many INT buffs you can fit on her)

Stack damage boosts

Recommended skills to cheese most Unit story stages and harder Story stages:

  1. Magic Aura (Skyship Lonvalion Lv10) for mages
  2. Sharp Eyes (Megrona Lv10) for physical DPS
  3. Pirate's Feast (Pirate Ship Legnis Lv10) for physical DPS
Etherion usage

Etherion priority:

  • Limited/Collab > Permanent
  • Red orb > BlueToRed > Blue
  • SSR arks > SR > R
Maximum Ark level for farming

(Soon to be deprecated) LV4 for R arks, LV3 for SR and SSR arks.

1-wave story stages

Gragia Mountains – Imperial Guard Commander 3

Drops green/blue crystals, green/blue clusters, EXP pots and skill books.

Strategies:

1. (Recommended) Mage with 2,000+ HP, Shining Zok(T3) or Abyss Gate(T3), instant-cast accessories and Magic Aura.
Put in 1st party slot to increase likelihood of targeting boss which is Light-weak. The mobs are weak to Dark and Thunder. You can use T2 spells like Galaxy if you are confident you can 1-shot with a single cast (requires a fully built mage or Maja Wand).
Avoid using Dark Matter.

2. Three mage setup with Holy Bolt + Poison Cloud (both non-timestop spells) and High Level Magic Chant equipped.

3. Physical DPS setup, preferably with AoE units like Randi. Avoid healers, tanks, and single target melee AI (i.e. Sevia).

If you cannot clear Imperial Guard Commander 3 consistently, do the other Imperial Guard Commander stages. They're all 1 wave stages with slightly worse drop rates.

Port Lidel – Assault on the Populace

Drops purple crystals.

The enemy is 3 Loug Mez, which has no element attribute weakness (until you cast Weak).

Strategies:

1. (Recommended) Physical DPS setup, preferably with Randi and/or other units with God Slayer equipped.

2. Any mage with Weak debuff equipped. The AI will usually cast Weak first, but there are times when Weak takes 20 seconds to cast.
In either case, the mage will dally and spend time doing regular attacks instead of casting a sure-kill spell.

White Laboratory – Laboratory Depths (Boss stage, Normal only)

Drops skill books and magic books.

Lanceveil has annoying lightning spells that can flinch-lock your units, so equip High Level Magic Chant and use time-stop T2 spells (i.e. Galaxy, Blizzard, Dark Matter) for instant-cast magic.

Unit story stages

Why unit story stages?

Most story unit stages are good for ether farming, because they are all 1-wave and have specific element weaknesses that can be exploited by mage AI.

Below are a sample of several unit story stages I farmed.
Most unit story stages have easy opponents, so go for stages that drop unit souls you need.

Sevia – Plans

Use a mage with Dark and Fire spells equipped.
For example, Popoi with Poison Cloud + Burst, or Dyne with Crimson Flare.

Drops blue, green and red crystals.

Dyne – The Sage's Grimoire

Use a mage with any T2 or T3 Dark spell equipped.
For example, Dyne with Poison Cloud or Dark Matter.

Shin – Changes

Use a mage with any T3 Dark spell equipped. Enemy Lukiel has no damage ultimate, and does very little damage. Can be 1HKO'd with an unbuffed Abyss Gate or Evil Gate.
